Course Description

Unlock the power of Google Ads with our comprehensive course designed for beginners and intermediate users. Whether youre looking to boost your business visibility or start a career in digital marketing, this course covers everything you need to know about creating, managing, and optimizing Google Ads campaigns. Learn step-by-step how to set up your account, create compelling ads, choose the right keywords, and utilize advanced features to maximize your ROI. With practical examples and hands-on exercises, youll gain the skills to run successful Google Ads campaigns and stay ahead of your competitors.

Learning Outcomes:

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamentals of Google Ads and its key concepts.
  • Set up and navigate a Google Ads account efficiently.
  • Create and manage effective ad campaigns tailored to your business goals.
  • Implement keyword match types and choose the right keywords for your ads.
  • Write compelling ad copy and utilize ad extensions to enhance your ads.
  • Design and create visual and interactive HTML ads that capture attention.
  • Develop and apply bidding strategies to maximize your ad spend.
  • Analyze competitors and leverage insights to optimize your advertising efforts.
