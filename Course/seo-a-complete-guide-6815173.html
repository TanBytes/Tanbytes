Course Description

This course provides an in-depth understanding of Search Engine Optimization (SEO). It covers everything from the basics to advanced techniques, making it suitable for beginners and experienced professionals alike. Learn how search engines work, conduct effective keyword research, optimize your website, write SEO-friendly articles, and utilize both on-page and off-page SEO strategies to improve your sites visibility and ranking.

Learning Outcomes:

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamental principles of SEO and its importance.
  • Know how search engines work and how they index and rank content.
  • Conduct effective keyword research and track keyword performance.
  • Implement on-page SEO strategies to optimize your website content.
  • Utilize Yoast SEO for enhancing on-page optimization.
  • Write and improve SEO-friendly articles that engage readers and rank well.
  • Understand the different types of links and how to build a strong backlink profile.
  • Apply off-page SEO strategies to increase your sites authority and traffic.
  • Execute technical SEO tasks to improve site performance and user experience.
  • Use schema markup to enhance search engine results and improve visibility.
