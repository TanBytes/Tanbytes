SHIPPING POLICY

Thank you for visiting tanbytes.com (the "Website"). This Shipping Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding the delivery of our digital products and services. By accessing and using our Website, you agree to the terms described in this Shipping Policy.

Digital Products and Services

All products and services offered on our Website are digital in nature. Upon purchase, you will receive access to these products and services either via email or through our Website. No physical products will be shipped.

Order Confirmation

1. Order Confirmation Email: After you place an order on our Website, you will receive an order confirmation email to the email address provided during the checkout process. This email will contain details of your purchase, including the product or service purchased, the price, and any applicable taxes.

2. Access to Digital Products and Services: Upon successful payment, you will receive an email containing instructions on how to access your purchased digital products or services. This may include a download link, login credentials, or other necessary information to access the content on our Website.

Delivery Methods

1. Email Delivery: Digital products and services will be delivered to you via the email address provided at the time of purchase. Please ensure that you provide a valid email address and check your inbox (including the spam/junk folder) for the order confirmation and access details.

2. Website Access: In some cases, access to digital products and services may be provided directly through our Website. You will receive instructions on how to log in and access your purchased content.

Delivery Time

1. Instant Delivery: Most digital products and services are available for instant access upon successful payment. You should receive the order confirmation and access details within minutes of completing your purchase.

2. Delayed Delivery: In rare cases, there may be a delay in processing your order. If you do not receive your order confirmation or access details within 48 hours, please contact our customer support at support@tanbytes.com for assistance.
